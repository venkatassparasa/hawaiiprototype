## Demo Flow Suggestions

### Beginner Flow (Basic Introduction)
1. "Hello" → Test greeting response
2. "What can you help me with?" → Test capabilities overview
3. "How do I register a TVR?" → Test basic registration knowledge
4. "What does it cost?" → Test fee information
5. "Thank you" → Test closing response

### Intermediate Flow (Property Owner Journey)
1. "I want to rent my property as a TVR. What do I need?"
2. "How do I find out if my property is eligible?"
3. "What is the ETA for registration completion once applied?"
4. "What taxes do I need to charge?"
5. "What are the occupancy limits?"

### Advanced Flow (Complex Scenarios)
1. "My property is in an R-1 zone. What are the requirements?"
2. "Do I need an NCUC? What does that involve?"
3. "How does TAT and GET work with Airbnb?"
4. "What happens if I get a violation notice?"
5. "How do I use the GIS portal to verify zoning?"

### Scope Testing Flow
1. "How do I pay my water bill?" → Should be out of scope
2. "What are the TVR rules?" → Should be in scope
3. "Where do I get a marriage license?" → Should be out of scope
4. "What's the TAT rate?" → Should be in scope

## Tips for Demo
- Start with simple questions to show basic functionality
- Progress to more complex questions to demonstrate depth
- Include at least one out-of-scope question to show scope enforcement
- Use property-specific scenarios to make it realistic
- Test different knowledge categories to show comprehensive coverage
- Have follow-up questions ready based on chatbot responses
